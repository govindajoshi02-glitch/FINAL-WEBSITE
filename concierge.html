How it works

We buy it. You receive it.

Six steps from your shopping request to your front door — handled entirely by our team.

1
Submit your request
Fill in the product name, store URL, size, colour, and quantity via your ShipMySpree dashboard. Include screenshots or links.
2
We send you a quote
Within 24 hours, we confirm item availability and send you a transparent quote — product price + our 5% fee + local shipping if applicable.
3
You pay upfront
100% advance payment required before we place the order. Pay securely via international card, PayPal, or bank transfer.
4
We purchase the item
Our India-based team places the order with the Indian retailer using local payment methods — cards, wallets, or UPI.
5
Arrives at your locker
The product is delivered to your India Virtual Address at our Mumbai warehouse. We notify you with photos the moment it arrives.
6
Ships to your door
Consolidate with other orders or ship immediately. Choose your courier and track your package all the way to your door.
