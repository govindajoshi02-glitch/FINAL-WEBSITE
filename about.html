Our story

Born from a real frustration

The idea started with a simple question: "Why is it so hard to buy something from an Indian website and get it delivered affordably abroad?"

High courier fees with no transparency. Customs clearance handled by agents you'd never heard of. Zero visibility after a package left the Indian seller. We eliminated the lack of accountability and hidden fees.

ShipMySpree was built to fix all of that — using the infrastructure and accountability of a real global logistics company, not a forwarding startup with a website and a hopeful attitude.
