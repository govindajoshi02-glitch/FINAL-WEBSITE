The math

Why consolidation
saves so much

International couriers charge a "first kilo" premium — the first 500g of any shipment costs significantly more per gram than subsequent weight. If you ship 5 packages separately, you pay that premium 5 times.

By combining everything into one box, you pay the first-kilo premium once. Add smart repacking to reduce volumetric weight, and the savings compound quickly.

Without consolidation (5 packages)~₹12,000
With consolidation (1 package)~₹4,300
You save: ~₹7,700 — that's a 64% reduction. Based on illustrative express rates to UAE for 5 x 1.2kg packages.
